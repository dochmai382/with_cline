About Måneskin

Måneskin is an Italian rock band formed in Rome in 2016. The band is composed of vocalist Damiano David, bassist Victoria De Angelis, guitarist Thomas Raggi, and drummer Ethan Torchio. Performing in the streets in their early days, they rose to prominence after finishing second in the eleventh season of the Italian talent show X Factor in 2017. Their international breakthrough came when the foursome won the Eurovision Song Contest 2021 for Italy with the song "Zitti e buoni".

About Måneskin

Måneskin's compelling blend of rock, alternative, and glam rock elements has captivated audiences worldwide. Their music often explores themes of rebellion, love, and societal issues, delivered with raw energy and passion.

The band's name, "Måneskin," is Danish for "moonlight," a tribute to bassist Victoria De Angelis's Danish heritage. It symbolizes the band's eclectic and somewhat mysterious musical style.

Since their Eurovision victory, Måneskin has continued to release chart-topping hits and perform sold-out shows globally, solidifying their status as one of the most exciting acts in contemporary rock music.
